Fire Emblem

    Fire Emblem is one of Nintendo top mainstream franchise with Pokemon and Mario. As it turns out, Fire Emblem is different from many Nintendo games. This would include the strategical role taken in controlling your units. What make this game amazing was that units in the game can die and permanently. Choices you make would decide in their Fates.
